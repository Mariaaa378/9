Arctic Monkeys

British group from Sheffield, 2002 – Present

Arctic Monkeys' musical style has been mainly described as indie-rock,garage-rock, post-pank, altermative rock and other. A key part of their sound, and one that translates across their whole discography, is Turner's intricate and often rapidly delivered lyrics, sung in a distinctive strong Sheffield accent that their music became famed for in their early years.

According to Vice, «in Britain at least, Arctic Monkeys have reached a point where they are too enormous, too beloved a force to truly fail» and are probably the UK's biggest, most culturally important band.
